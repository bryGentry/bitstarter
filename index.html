YourTour Mapmaker is the easiest way to make a custom interactive map that serves as a virtual tour that guides current visitors and entices future ones.

Place video, photos, and other content on the map so they appear when the user clicks a point of interest.

Here are just a few uses:

  • Make a walking tour of your downtown guiding visitors to restaurants, historic sites, and shopping destinations.
  • Guide visitors (or prospective visitors) to buildings and sites on your school campus.
  • Lead homebuyers on a parade of homes you have for sale.
  • Highlight tourist attractions throughout your region

YourTour Mapmaker will creatively apply new web technologies to make the entire experience, from making the map to using it on a tour, easy and enjoyable.

Here are just a few features:

  • Location Aware. YourTour maps know where users are, allowing them to display the map accordingly. Perfect for maps that people might use to get from point of interest to another.
  • A cinch to use. Making a map is as easy as giving it a name and defining the location to display. Then add points of interest by choosing their names and locations.
  • Custom Content. For each point, you can add videos, photos, or pictures to display at those locations.
  • Next and Previous buttons. You can place your tour stops in an order, allowing map users to go from your location to the spot you would like for them to visit next.
  • Custom images. You can select a customized image or marker, such as a sketch of a building or a logo, for each point on the map. Also, it is easy to overlay a custom illustrated map on top of the interactive map.
